Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sean's Tools (tools.imseankim.com) handles information when you use our website and tools.

Client-Side Processing

All tools on this site run entirely in your browser. Your files, text, images, and any other input are processed locally on your device using client-side JavaScript. No data you provide to our tools is uploaded to, stored on, or transmitted to any external server.

No User Accounts or Personal Data Collection

We do not require user accounts, logins, or registration of any kind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details through our tools.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing activity.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • For more information on how Google uses data, please review Google's Advertising Policy.

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about site traffic, such as page views, visit duration, and general geographic location. This information helps us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve our tools and user experience. Google Analytics may use cookies to gather this data. No personally identifiable information is collected through this service.

Cookies

This site does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services integrated into this site — specifically Google AdSense and Google Analytics — may place cookies on your device. These cookies are used for:

  • Serving and personalizing advertisements
  • Analyzing site traffic and usage patterns

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of ads displayed on the site.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only third-party services that may receive anonymized or cookie-based data are Google AdSense and Google Analytics, as described above.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
